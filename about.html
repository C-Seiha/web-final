About Our Project

Our natural sponges are more durable and do not smell. Large perforations in the sponge shape enable speedy drying and lessen the likelihood of bacterial growth.

Sponges are helpful for the environment since they pick up rubbish and minimize it. Because it absorbs water well and breaks down into fertilizer, you can chop it up after usage and use it on your land.

Eco-friendly packaging choices Instead of using plastic, go for eco-friendly packaging like paper or boxes made from palm leaves.
